О Overwatch Item Tracker

Overwatch Item Tracker is a website designed to let you track new items added to Overwatch for each event. It allows you to mark items as selected so you know what you have collected and what you still need to collect, it also shows previews of all the new items apart from voice lines


Initial Design by Lalato & Izzepizze on Reddit, разработал сайт — Js41637, а перевёл на русский язык — DFPX
